# ForecastIQ Presentation Guide

## 5 Minute Pitch Structure

1. Problem
   - Ecommerce teams need budget decisions before outcomes are known.
   - Static dashboards do not provide forecast uncertainty, simulation, or recommended actions.

2. Solution
   - ForecastIQ validates data, forecasts revenue and ROAS, simulates budgets, detects risks and opportunities, and generates executive insights.

3. Technical Differentiation
   - FastAPI backend with typed Pydantic contracts.
   - Committed scikit-learn artifact with horizon champion-challenger selection.
   - Lag, rolling, seasonality, and media-volume feature engineering.
   - Forecast accuracy dashboard with MAE, RMSE, MAPE, and R2.
   - Explainability center with feature importance and driver explanations.
   - Decision intelligence for budgets, scenarios, risk, opportunity, and health scores.

4. Business Value
   - Faster weekly/monthly media planning.
   - More disciplined budget reallocation.
   - Better executive communication through PDF-ready summaries.
   - Clear confidence intervals for conservative and upside planning.

5. Demo Close
   - "This is not just a forecast chart. It is a decision workflow from raw campaign data to board-ready action."

## Presentation Tips

- Lead with business impact, not technology names.
- Show the full workflow in under 3 minutes.
- Use confidence intervals to discuss responsible forecasting.
- Explain fallback behavior so judges know the app is demo-safe.
- End with measurable next steps: deployment, CI/CD, external demand signals, model monitoring.
